What is the LRC Editor?

It is a specialized tool for creating and editing LRC files. Unlike traditional text-based editors, it uses a visual timeline and "chunk-based" syncing to make timing lyrics faster and more precise.

How does the lyric chunker work?

The chunker allows you to split lyrics into logical segments (chunks) and drag them onto a timeline. You can then fine-tune their start and end times relative to the audio waveform.

Is this tool free and private?

Yes. The application runs entirely in your browser using WebAssembly. Your audio files and lyric data never leave your computer, ensuring complete privacy and offline capability.

Which formats are supported?

You can import common audio formats (MP3, WAV, OGG) and import/export standard .lrc files compatible with most music players and karaoke systems.
