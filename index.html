What is an LRC Editor?

An LRC editor is a tool used to create and synchronize lyrics with audio files. This free web-based LRC maker allows you to generate timed lyrics for songs, making it perfect for karaoke, music videos, and lyric-synchronized music players. Unlike basic text editors, this tool provides a visual timeline for precise timing.

How do I synchronize lyrics for free?

You can synchronize lyrics for free using our intuitive chunk-based lyric creator. Simply paste your lyrics, split them into chunks, and drag them onto the audio timeline. You can adjust the start and end times of every word or phrase with millisecond precision without downloading any software.

Is this LRC maker private and secure?

Yes. This application runs entirely in your browser using WebAssembly. Your audio files and lyric data never leave your computer, ensuring 100% privacy. It works offline once loaded and is a secure alternative to online tools that require file uploads.

Where can I use .lrc files?

LRC files created here are compatible with all major music players and karaoke systems that support timed lyrics. You can use them in mobile music apps, desktop players, and even for creating lyric videos on social media platforms.

Which audio formats are supported?

Our web-based tool supports common audio formats including MP3, WAV, and OGG. You can import your music file and immediately start creating synchronized timed lyrics in your browser.
